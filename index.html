What Is a Living Book?

It's not a book. It's not a course.
It's not a coaching program.
It's all three — running automatically while you sleep.

Most books are purchased once and forgotten. The Living Book is different. Readers don't just read your ideas — they interact with them, apply them to their specific situation, and come back month after month because the relationship with your thinking has become too valuable to cancel.
